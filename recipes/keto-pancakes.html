Keto Pancakes

Description

This Carbquik recipe yields 3 Net Carbs per severing.
Add additional butter for higher fat macros.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups CarbquikÂ®
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup butter melted
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk all the ingredients until fully combined. Batter should be the same consistency as regular pancake batter.
  2. Heat skillet or griddle to medium heat. Lightly grease with butter and drop the batter onto the hot skillet, forming small circles. Cook 2 minutes per side or until golden and cooked through.
